Turkish Philanthropy Funds via NYU Turkish Students Association

Funds go directly to US based aid foundation “Turkish Philanthropy Funds”.

AKUT Foundation

NGO for rescue operations in Turkey currently working actively in the affected region. Accepts payments with credit/bank cards.

AHBAP

Turkey based aid organization that is currently active in the region. Accepts donations via SWIFT transfers.

AFAD

Turkey's official disaster and emergency authority. Accepts donations via SWIFT transfers.

Oxfam

UK based global aid foundation.

Turkey Mozaik Foundation

Founded by Turkish migrants living in the UK. Boğaziçi University alumni are among them.

Red Cross Austria

The webpage is in German.

Bündnis Entwicklung Hilft & Aktion Deutschland Hilft

The webpage is in German. The foundations are known aid platforms in Germany.

Verein zur Förderung der Zeitgemäßen Lebensweise

The German chapter of the known Turkish charity “Çağdaş Yaşamı Destekleme Derneği”.
